## 2024-03-05 - Extracted StatelessWidgets do not always yield massive microbenchmark wins, but are correct Flutter architecture
**Learning:** In Flutter, extracting methods that return widgets (like `_buildLawCard` or `_buildLawHeader`) into `const StatelessWidget`s is a highly recommended practice to reduce unnecessary rebuilds of complex widget subtrees when a parent widget calls `setState()`. Our test benchmark only saw a ~15% improvement, but conceptually this scales much better. Additionally, moving the `TextEditingController` initialization from inline in `build()` to `initState()` avoids creating a new controller and losing focus state on every rebuild, preventing memory leaks and interaction bugs.
**Action:** Use `const StatelessWidget` for localized helper methods, and ensure `TextEditingController`s are correctly managed in State lifecycle methods (`initState` and `dispose`).
